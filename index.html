Bispit

Bispit is a user-friendly app designed to streamline and simplify expense sharing among friends, family, and roommates. Perfect for vacations, shared households, or group activities, Bispit allows users to easily track and divide costs, ensuring everyone pays their fair share. With intuitive features for recording expenses, splitting bills, and settling up, Bispit takes the hassle out of managing group finances, making it effortless to keep track of who owes what. Enjoy your time together without worrying about the details, and let Bispit handle the numbers for you.
